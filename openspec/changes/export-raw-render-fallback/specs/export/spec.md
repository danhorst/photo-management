## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Derivative source resolution

The system SHALL resolve, for each frame, one base derivative source plus one source per baked
edit. The base source SHALL be the sibling camera JPEG, or the master (RAW) when the frame is
RAW-only. A RAW-only base SHALL be realized from the master's embedded full-resolution JPEG
(`PreviewImage`, else `JpgFromRaw`) when present, and SHALL fall back to a direct render of the
master when it carries no embedded JPEG. The fallback SHALL NOT change the base source's identity:
the version id remains the master's content hash and the recorded `source_kind` stays `embedded`.
Edit sources SHALL be every `<stem>-<suffix>` baked
file. Edits are additive and SHALL NOT suppress the base. An iPhone-origin frame (a `<stem>.HEIC`
with no camera JPEG) SHALL yield no derivative.

#### Scenario: RAF+JPEG frame with two edits

- **WHEN** a frame has a camera JPEG and two baked edits `-edit` and `-bw`
- **THEN** three sources resolve: the camera JPEG (base) plus the `-edit` and `-bw` files

#### Scenario: RAW-only frame uses embedded JPEG

- **WHEN** a frame has a RAW file with an embedded full-resolution JPEG but no sibling camera JPEG
- **THEN** the base source is realized from the RAW's embedded full-resolution JPEG (`PreviewImage`, else `JpgFromRaw`)

#### Scenario: RAW-only frame with no embedded JPEG renders directly

- **WHEN** a frame's RAW master carries no embedded full-resolution JPEG (e.g. a Linear Raw DNG, or a JPEG misnamed with a RAW extension)
- **THEN** the base derivative is generated by rendering the master directly, and the frame exports rather than failing with `no embedded JPEG`

#### Scenario: iPhone-origin frame is left alone

- **WHEN** a frame is a `<stem>.HEIC` with no camera JPEG
- **THEN** no derivative source is resolved and the frame is skipped
